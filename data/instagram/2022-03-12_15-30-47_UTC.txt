Frédérique Turgeon @fredturgeon flies down the hill with one goal in mind: winning. Flying out from Québec is a different story for the @cdnparalympics and Air Canada athlete. Frédéric dreams of a variety of destinations, from training in St. Moritz, Switzerland, to vacationing on the beaches of Maine, and would consider trading the snowy hills of Bromont for the sandy beaches of Hawaii. 

As you may have learned already, Frédérique suffered an injury during training in Beijing and will unfortunately not be able to compete. Join us as we wish her all the best for a speedy recovery.
