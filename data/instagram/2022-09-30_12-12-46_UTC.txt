Today on the second National Day for Truth and Reconciliation, we listen and reflect to learn more about the tragic, painful history and ongoing impact of residential schools.
 
Three of our colleagues bravely share their experiences as Indigenous people.

Filmed at Spotted Lake, a sacred place of healing, James Hemsworth, Senior Technician, Aircraft Performance, Karen Chapman, Service Director, and Inez Cook, retired Flight Attendant open up about what truth and reconciliation means to them.
