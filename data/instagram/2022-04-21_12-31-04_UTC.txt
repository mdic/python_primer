We’re pleased to reintroduce our Air Canada Chauffeur Service at Toronto Pearson International Airport, in partnership with one of the world’s most iconic luxury car brands, @porschecanada. 

Starting this summer, eligible Air Canada Signature Class customers connecting internationally will be whisked away on the tarmac in a Porsche electric or plug-in hybrid electric vehicle for their visit to the exclusive Air Canada Signature Suite.
